Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

At B.Tech Walleh, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Google Drive Integration

When you connect your Google Drive account using the "Sign in with Google" feature, we request access to specific Google Drive scopes to allow saving and retrieving your code files. The scopes requested are limited to the minimum required functionality (read/write access to application-specific files).

We do not request access to your Gmail, contacts, or other unrelated Google services.

Data We Access and Store

  • Files you create or save through the compiler feature (code files).
  • Basic profile information required for login (name, email, avatar) as provided by Google.

We do not store your Google account password. OAuth tokens may be stored securely to maintain your session and allow convenient file access; you can revoke access from your Google account at any time.

Purpose and Use

Data accessed from Google Drive is used only to save, load, and manage your code projects within the B.Tech Walleh web application. We will not sell or share your personal files with third parties.

Retention

Your files remain in your Google Drive storage. If we store any metadata (for performance or indexing), it is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service unless you request deletion.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data. OAuth tokens and any stored metadata are handled securely. However, no internet transmission can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may revoke access to your Google Drive at any time via your Google Account > Security > Third-party apps with account access. To request deletion of any metadata we may have stored, contact us at developer@btechwalleh.com.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at developer@btechwalleh.com.
